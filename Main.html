Palliative Care National Rate Analysis Project

This project explores the national rates for various palliative care measures in Pediatric Critical Care Hospitals (PCHs) using data obtained from the Medicare.gov Hospital Compare portal.

Project Overview

The primary goal is to understand the different types of end-of-life care measures being tracked at a national level and to analyze the reported national rates for these measures.

Dataset

The dataset used is 'PCH_Palliative_Care_NATIONAL.csv', which provides national-level aggregated data for specific palliative care metrics.
