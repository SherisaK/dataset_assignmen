Visualizations of Palliative Care National Rates

This section displays visualizations created to understand the national rates for different palliative care measures.

National Rates by Measure

This bar plot shows the national rate for each reported palliative care measure. The x-axis represents the Measure ID, and the y-axis represents the National Rate.

Distribution of National Rates

This histogram illustrates the distribution of the national rates across all the reported palliative care measures. It shows the frequency of different rate values.

Box Plot of National Rates

This box plot provides a summary of the distribution of national rates, showing the median, quartiles, and potential outliers.
